METAMORPHOSIS

A profound change in form from one stage to the next

"Metamorphosis" by Nevena Kostic

Metamorphosis (meta- "change" + morphe "form") as a biological process is generally attributed to a subset of animals: most famously insects and amphibians, but some fish and many marine invertebrates as well. The most basic example of this would be the metamorphosis of a caterpillar into a butterfly.
